# DrivePinas — Impeccable Context

## Project
- **Name**: DrivePinas
- **Type**: Car Showcase / Dealership Landing Page
- **Tagline**: Drive Your Journey
- **Tech**: Single HTML file, vanilla CSS + JS, no frameworks

## Target Audience
- Filipino car buyers (25–45 age range)
- Mobile-first users (majority browse on phones)
- Browsing pre-owned cars from trusted brands
- Price-conscious but value quality and transparency
- Communicate primarily via Facebook Messenger, Viber, phone calls

## Use Cases
1. Browse available cars by brand
2. View full details / photos of a specific unit
3. Check pricing and condition
4. Contact the dealer for inquiries

## Brand Personality
- **Professional** — Not flashy or gimmicky, inspires trust
- **Bold** — Black + Red identity, confident and assertive
- **Transparent** — Every detail visible, no hidden info
- **Modern** — Clean lines, contemporary design, not dated

## Logo Analysis
- Monogram: **DF** with wing motif on the "F" (suggests speed, dynamism)
- Colors: **Black (#1A1A1A)** + **Red (#E31837)** on white
- Typography: Bold, italic sans-serif — automotive/motorsport energy
- Tagline: "DRIVE YOUR JOURNEY" — spaced uppercase, light weight

## Design Theme
- **Two-Tone Split**: Dark hero/header/footer + white content areas
- Dark sections: `#1A1A1A` (matches logo black)
- Accent: `#E31837` (matches logo red)
- Content: `#FFFFFF` / `#F5F5F5`
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)

## Quality Bar
- Must feel like a real dealership website, not a student project
- Photos should be prominent — cars are the product
- Price and specs must be instantly scannable
- Mobile experience is primary, desktop is secondary
- Animations should feel automotive-premium, not playful
